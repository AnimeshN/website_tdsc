Services

TDSC offers technical consulting services on projects serving the bottom 80% in the basic sectors of water, energy, sanitation, healthcare and agriculture. For existing systems, we offer status assessment, redesign, monitoring and evaluation services. For planned/new projects, we offer need-assessment, techno-economic feasibility study, detailed design, and monitoring of implementation. An additional objective of TDSC is to help develop a knowledge network of regional institutions working in the development sector. TDSC's outreach parallel will involve dissemination of outputs such as protocols and case studies generated through TDSC projects and live research. We aim to provide services to all groups engaged in the basic development sectors:

  • Gram panchayats, zilla parishads, district planning committees
  • Municipal corporations and other urban local bodies
  • Central and state government ministries and other policy makers
  • NGOs, trusts, self-help groups and citizen groups, and other community-based organizations
  • CSR divisions of corporates
  • Academic institutions
